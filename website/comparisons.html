Compare honestly, measure repeatably.

Loom should be compared against Safe, Kernel, Nexus, Coinbase Smart Wallet, Clave, ZeroDev, Biconomy, and other account stacks. This page separates implemented Loom behavior from benchmark data that still needs live, reproducible measurement.

Feature comparison

Capability Loom Typical ecosystem pattern
Upgrade authority No proxy admin, no factory authority, delayed migration instead. Varies: immutable accounts, modular accounts, or upgradeable systems.
Passkey auth P-256/WebAuthn and multi-passkey threshold validator. Increasingly common in passkey-first wallets.
Social recovery Guardian threshold, visible delay, expiry, cancellation, fresh guardian root. Common in social recovery wallets, implementation details vary.
Permissions Granular target, selector, token, amount, time, use count, and paymaster bounds. Session keys and Smart Sessions are common, often broader in surface.
Privacy Kohaku-backed SDK boundary with provider consent and metadata budgets; concrete adapters pending. Usually wallet-side or protocol-specific, rarely core account logic.
Cross-chain authority L1 keystore surface and proof-gated sync module; production proof verifiers pending. Often service, bridge, or chain-specific; trust assumptions vary.

Gas comparison framework

Cross-wallet gas numbers are not published here until the same benchmark harness runs against the same compiler settings, networks, EntryPoint version, bundler path, and operation shape. Loom currently publishes its own gas snapshot in the repository.
